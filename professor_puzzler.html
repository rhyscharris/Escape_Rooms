Professor Puzzler

Professor Puzzler's Underground Laboratory has many puzzles and clues to find before the chest of secrets can be unlocked. Children from 7 to 11 can take part, with puzzles suitable for all. Sessions last for 30 minutes in the purpose built activity room, providing hands-on challenges to solve Professor Puzzler's secrets. Whether as a stand-alone activity for between 2 or 6 children or a party, combined with our other activities, our new junior escape room is sure to delight.
